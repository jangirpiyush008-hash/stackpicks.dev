## FOUNDING SPONSOR PRICING STRATEGY

Months 1-3 (low traffic): Offer ALL tiers at 50% off as "Founding Sponsor."
- Category top: ₹1,250/mo (instead of ₹2,500)
- Homepage: ₹5,000/mo (instead of ₹10,000)
- Newsletter: ₹2,500/send (instead of ₹5,000)

Lock in 6-month minimum. Convert at full rate after 6 months OR when you can show 10x traffic growth.

This is how every directory does it. Don't price for what you wish your traffic was. Price for what you have, with a multiplier for early commitment.

---

## NO REPLIES? HERE'S WHY

Cold outreach has a 1-5% reply rate. Plan for 50+ emails to get 3-5 sponsors. Reasons for no-reply:

1. They opened it, weren't interested, didn't bother replying — most common
2. Email went to spam — check from a deliverability tool like mail-tester.com
3. They forwarded internally and it's in someone's queue
4. Your traffic numbers aren't compelling yet — wait a month

Don't take silence personally. Send the next 10 emails.

## REPLY PLAYBOOK

**They say "interested, send details":**
→ Send the one-pager above. Don't ask for a call.

**They say "too expensive":**
→ Reply: "Totally fair. Want to start with the newsletter slot at ₹5k? Lower commitment, see the audience first."

**They say "send me a deck":**
→ Reply: "No deck — single page. {{one-pager URL}}. Decks make this longer than it needs to be."

**They say "let's do a call":**
→ Reply: "Happy to. 15 mins works? Or — quicker — what's the question I can answer over email?" (Forces them to commit time OR ask the real question.)

**They go silent after the one-pager:**
→ Day 4 follow-up: "Quick check — did this make sense or is there a question I missed?"
→ Day 14 close: "Closing the loop — I'll keep your contact for the next quarterly outreach. {{One specific thing about their product}} — looks good. Cheers."
