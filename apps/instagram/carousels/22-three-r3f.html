Three Shading Language is what GLSL should've been.
TSL lets you write shaders in JS — same expressiveness as GLSL, but typed, composable, and transpiled to both WebGL and WebGPU automatically. No more separate .glsl files.
→ JS in · WebGL+WebGPU out
