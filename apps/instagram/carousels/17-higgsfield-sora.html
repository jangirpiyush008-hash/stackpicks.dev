01 · pattern
Pick the shot before the scene.
Most Sora prompts fail because they describe what's happening, not how the camera sees it. Pick the camera move first: dolly, orbit, push-in, crash-zoom. Then describe what's in frame.
→ "slow orbit around" > "a person near"

03 · prompt
The 3-part formula.
1) Camera move ('slow dolly-in on...'). 2) Subject + action ('a barista pulling an espresso shot'). 3) Lighting + mood ('warm morning window light, shallow focus').
→ keep it under 50 words
