# Monetization Playbook

Realistic revenue projections for StackPicks based on traffic milestones.
All amounts in INR.

## The 5 rails (wired in v1)

### 1. Sponsored listings (highest revenue lever)

| Placement | Price/month | Inventory | Max monthly potential |
|---|---|---|---|
| Homepage featured | ₹10,000 | 3 slots | ₹30,000 |
| Category top | ₹2,500 | 22 categories × 2 slots | ₹1,10,000 |
| Newsletter slot | ₹5,000/send | 4 sends/month | ₹20,000 |

**Max theoretical:** ₹1,60,000/month at full sellout. Realistic at 10k MAU: 30–40% fill = ₹50–65k/month.

**Pricing strategy:** Start at 50% these prices for first 3 sponsors as "founding sponsor" deals. Raise once you have a case study + traffic proof.

**Where to find sponsors:** Reach out to founders of tools you list. Cold DM the founder on Twitter/LinkedIn with: "We send X visitors/month to your category. ₹2,500 puts you at the top." 5% reply rate, 1% close rate is normal.

### 2. Affiliate revenue

Best partners for a dev tool directory:

- **Vercel:** referral credits, not cash, but compounds for your own usage
- **Supabase:** partner program for referrals
- **Hostinger:** ₹500–2,000 per signup (India angle)
- **Frontend Masters:** 30% recurring
- **Codecademy:** 50% first month
- **Razorpay:** referral bonuses

Realistic at 10k MAU: ₹15,000–30,000/month with 5–8 active affiliate partners.

### 3. Premium tier (₹299/mo)

What's gated:
- Weekly deep-dive picks ("Stack of the Week" with 2,000-word analysis)
- Full collections (curated stacks for specific use cases)
- Members-only Discord
- Newsletter without sponsor blocks
- Early access to new categories/features

**Conversion benchmark for builder audiences:** 0.5–1.5% of MAU.
At 10k MAU = 50–150 subscribers = ₹15,000–45,000/month.

**Critical:** Premium only works if free tier already feels valuable. Don't gate too aggressively or you kill SEO.

### 4. Newsletter sponsorships

Build the list first. Once you cross 1,000 confirmed subscribers:

- 1k–5k subs: ₹3,000–₹8,000 per send
- 5k–10k subs: ₹10,000–₹20,000 per send
- 10k+ subs: ₹25,000+ per send

Weekly cadence = 4 sends/month. At 5k subs with 50% sellout = ₹20,000/month.

### 5. Job board (₹5,000 per 30-day listing)

Phase 2 — flip the switch once you have ~5k MAU. Realistic:
- Month 1–3 of activation: 2–5 listings/month = ₹10,000–₹25,000
- After: 8–15 listings/month = ₹40,000–₹75,000

## Hidden revenue: lead gen for your services

Your real edge: StackPicks becomes a top-of-funnel for **your freelance/consulting work**. People reading "use shadcn if you need X" content will want to hire someone who knows shadcn — that's you. One ₹3 lakh project from the audience pays for hosting forever.

Add a subtle "Need help building? I take on 2 clients/quarter" link in the footer once you have traction. Don't be aggressive about it.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't run display ads** (AdSense, Carbon). Cheapens the brand, low revenue per visitor for dev audiences.
- **Don't gate the directory itself.** Free access is the whole reason people come.
- **Don't fake sponsored slots** by buying them yourself. Wait for real demand.
- **Don't take ₹500/month sponsors** to "fill inventory." Low prices anchor expectations.
- **Don't promise SEO bumps to sponsors.** That's a Google policy violation that tanks your domain.

## Sponsor sales script

Cold email to tool founders once you have traffic:

> Subject: Featured slot on StackPicks ({{their category}})
>
> Hey {{name}},
>
> Quick one: StackPicks (the curated open-source directory) gets {{X}} visitors/month, with {{Y}}/month landing on the {{category}} page where {{their tool}} would go.
>
> The top slot is ₹2,500/month. Includes one paragraph of dedicated copy and your logo above the organic listings. Average click-through is 4–6%.
>
> Want me to send the asset spec?
>
> — Piyush
> Founder, StackPicks

Don't be cute. Don't over-explain. Founders are busy and they can do the math themselves.
